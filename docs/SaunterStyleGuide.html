Design Principles

The Saunter theme treats every link post like an editorial dispatch. The interface leans on system-friendly typography, a reduced grayscale palette, and purposeful blue accents.

  • Calm surface, bold signals: muted background with bright electric blue for actions and emphasis.
  • Content-first: card layouts avoid thumbnails unless the post provides one, and detail pages push metadata beneath the fold.
  • Micro.blog aware: category badges, RSS hooks, and draft vs. published cues mirror the sync engine‚Äôs states.
  • Time-aware theming: header toggle plus automatic light/dark logic keep the experience aligned with device preference or local time.

Usage Guidelines

Do

  • Keep posts un-truncated‚Äîlet micro.blog‚Äôs Markdown shine.
  • Use the category badges to group content from Pinboard tags.
  • Embed only essential links in the footer (colophon + RSS).
  • Lean on Fraunces for headings and Inter for everything else.

Don't

  • Introduce new accent colors; Sky/Ocean blue are the only ones.
  • Outline cards with drop shadows‚Äîstick to single-pixel lines.
  • Add title fields to micro.blog posts; Saunter expects titleless entries.
  • Break the variable naming conventions when porting to Hugo.
